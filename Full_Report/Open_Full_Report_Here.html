This comprehensive analysis tackles a critical retail challenge: how to allocate marketing resources efficiently when a small core of Premium Loyalists generates 76% of revenue, while the broader customer base remains underpenetrated.

Using the Dunnhumby Complete Journey dataset (2,500 households, ~2.6M transactions over 711 days), we built an integrated strategy across five analytical modules that answer interconnected business questions about segmentation, bundling, pricing, targeting, and retention.
